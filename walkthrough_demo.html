Property Registration Flow

1

Validate Identity

Checks if the caller has a verified identity and meets the minimum reputation score.

2

Compliance Check

Ensures the account is compliant with regional regulations via the Compliance Registry.

3

State Update

Increments property counter and maps metadata to the new unique Property ID.

4

Emit Events

Publishes a PropertyRegistered event for off-chain indexing and audit logging.
